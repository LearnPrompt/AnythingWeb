Chinese media was quick to tout Manus as an AI breakthrough; [publication QQ News called it](https://news.qq.com/rain/a/20250308A05UNR00?uid%5B0%5D=2103349820&uid%5B1%5D=2103349820&suid=&media_id=) ‚Äúthe pride of domestic products.‚Äù Meanwhile, AI influencers on social media spread misinformation about Manus‚Äô capabilities. Butterfly Effect didn‚Äôt develop models in-house, unlike DeepSeek.
